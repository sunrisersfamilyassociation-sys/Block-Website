Apartment Policies & Byelaws

General Rules

  • Maintain cleanliness in common areas.
  • Noise levels should be kept minimal after 10 PM.
  • Pets must be kept on a leash in common areas.

Maintenance

  • Monthly maintenance fees must be paid by the 5th of each month.
  • Any damage caused by residents must be repaired at their expense.

Parking

  • Parking spots are allocated on a first-come basis.
  • Visitors must park in designated visitor areas only.
